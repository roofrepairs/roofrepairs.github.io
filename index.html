Common Problems

TYPES OF ROOF ISSUES THAT WE FIX

Nairobi's tropical climate — with heavy long and short rains, intense UV and temperature fluctuations — creates a range of specific roofing problems. Our experts resolve all of them.

💧

Roof Leaks

Water ingress through damaged tiles, cracked flashings or failed membranes — the most common call-out for Roof Repair in Nairobi.

🦠

Moss & Algae Growth

Nairobi's humid rainy seasons promote rapid moss and algae growth that retains moisture and degrades roofing materials prematurely.

🌊

Storm & Wind Damage

High winds lift tiles, sheets and ridges. Hailstorms puncture membranes. We respond quickly to storm damage across Nairobi.

🔩

Loose / Missing Tiles & Sheets

Corroded screws and aged tiles detach over time, creating entry points for water and reducing structural integrity.

📉

Sagging & Structural Failure

Rotted rafters, weakened trusses or waterlogged decking cause dangerous sags that require urgent structural roof repair.

🧱

Flashing Failures

Corroded or improperly installed chimney, skylight and valley flashings are leading causes of persistent roof leaks.

🌡️

Thermal Cracking

Nairobi's temperature swings cause flat roof membranes and coatings to crack and blister, requiring waterproofing repair.

🚿

Blocked Gutters & Drains

Debris-clogged gutters cause water to back up under roof edges, rotting fascias and causing ceiling leaks.

Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ABOUT ROOF REPAIR IN NAIROBI

Everything you need to know before booking your Roof Repair in Nairobi with Bestcare Facilities Management.

How much does roof repair cost in Nairobi?

Roof repair costs in Nairobi vary by scope. Minor repairs (small leaks, single tile) start from KSh 5,000–15,000. Moderate repairs range from KSh 20,000–80,000. Full replacements for a standard 3-bedroom house range from KSh 150,000–500,000+. We provide free assessments and detailed quotations before any work begins.

How do I know if my roof needs repair or full replacement?

If the roof is under 15 years old and damage is localised, repairs are usually sufficient. Widespread water damage, structural sagging or roofs over 20 years old may warrant full replacement. Our inspectors provide honest, unbiased advice on the best option for your specific situation and budget.

Do you offer emergency roof repair in Nairobi?

Yes. We provide 24/7 emergency roof repair across Nairobi, Kiambu and Kajiado. Call 0709004600 any time. We deploy within 1–4 hours for urgent situations including storm damage, sudden structural failure and major leaks during heavy rain.

What types of roofs do you repair in Nairobi?

We repair all roof types: corrugated and box-profile iron sheets (mabati), clay tiles, concrete tiles, flat bitumen roofs, TPO and EPDM membrane roofs, metal roofing systems, slate, fibre cement, and all types of commercial flat roofing used in Nairobi's offices, warehouses and shopping centres.

How long does roof repair take in Nairobi?

Small repairs are typically completed in 1 working day. Moderate repairs take 2–5 days. Full roof replacements on standard 3-bedroom homes take 5–10 working days. We provide a clear timeline in our quotation and keep you updated throughout the project.

Can you repair a leaking roof during the rainy season in Nairobi?

Yes. We use weatherproof temporary sheeting to protect your property while work proceeds. We also plan repair schedules around weather forecasts. Some permanent repairs require dry conditions, but we stage work efficiently to minimise disruption during both the long and short rains in Nairobi.

Do you provide a warranty on roof repairs in Nairobi?

Yes. All repairs carry a 1–2 year labour warranty. Full replacements are backed by a 5-year workmanship warranty and up to 25-year manufacturer material warranties where applicable. We stand behind every job we do in Nairobi.

What areas in Nairobi do you cover for roof repair?

We cover all Nairobi areas including Westlands, Karen, Kilimani, Lavington, Parklands, Runda, Muthaiga, Gigiri, Kileleshwa, CBD, Upper Hill, South B & C, Eastleigh, Kasarani, Embakasi, and all of Kiambu and Kajiado counties.

Do I need a permit for roof repair or replacement in Nairobi?

Minor repairs generally do not require permits. Full replacements and structural changes typically need Nairobi City County building approval. Bestcare handles all permit acquisition, documentation and code compliance on your behalf as part of our full-service offering.

How can I prevent future roof problems in Nairobi?

Schedule annual roof inspections before the long rains, keep gutters clean, apply waterproofing coatings every 3–5 years, address minor issues promptly and ensure adequate ventilation. Bestcare offers subscription maintenance programmes that cover all of this for a predictable annual cost.
