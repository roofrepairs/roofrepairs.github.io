## Common Roofing Problems

Property owners frequently encounter several roofing issues that require immediate attention:

* Leaking roofs due to broken or displaced tiles
* Rusting and corrosion on metal sheets
* Poor drainage leading to water pooling
* Damaged flashing around chimneys and vents
* Structural sagging caused by prolonged water damage

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs and interior damage.
